## Mods
How do they work.

Basically if you gonna hover mod it will show what does this mod do.

as you can see mods are splitted into categories.

So the first one decreases difficulty but also score multipler which means you will get less score.

The 2nd one Increases you diffculty and score multipler.

and the last one which is unranked mods meaning your score won't be ranked and will not be counted

(on titanic with Relax and autopilot it is ranked but with 0.5X multipler)

## Skins
Bored of default skin?

Here comes the help of community which makes custom skins.

To install one:
1. close game
2. Place .osk file inside Skins folder
3. Start game
4. Open settings and tab Skin
5. Click the current skin name
6. Select your skin from the list

(this tutorial may be wrong for some game versions)
